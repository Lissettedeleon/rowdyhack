Dinosaurs Information

6. Spinosaurus

Diet: Carnivore, believed to be primarily fish

Enemies: Carcharodontosaurus

Flight: No

Interesting Fact: Spinosaurus is the largest carnivorous dinosaur ever discovered, even larger than Tyrannosaurus Rex.

7. Ankylosaurus

Diet: Herbivore

Enemies: Tyrannosaurus Rex

Flight: No

Interesting Fact: Ankylosaurus had armor plates embedded in its skin and a massive club at the end of its tail for defense.

8. Pterodactyl (Pteranodon)

Diet: Carnivore, mainly fish

Enemies: Larger predatory dinosaurs and marine reptiles

Flight: Yes

Interesting Fact: Despite being popularly known, Pterodactyls are not dinosaurs but flying reptiles that lived during the time of dinosaurs.

9. Diplodocus

Diet: Herbivore

Enemies: Allosaurus and other predators of its time

Flight: No

Interesting Fact: Diplodocus had a whip-like tail it could snap, potentially as a sound signal or defense mechanism.

11. Pteranodon

Diet: Mainly fish

Enemies: Larger marine reptiles and larger pterosaurs

Flight: Yes, one of the largest flying reptiles with a wingspan up to 20 feet.

Interesting Fact: Despite its size, Pteranodon had a relatively small body and long, pointed beak.
